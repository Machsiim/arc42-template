== Introduction and Goals

The Image Sharing App is a mobile platform targeting photography enthusiasts aged 18–35. It enables users to share, discover, and edit high-quality images with advanced tools, customizable filters, and a strong community aspect. The app differentiates itself through Pixlr integration for cloud-based photo editing and a freemium subscription model.

=== Quality Goals

The top quality goals for the architecture, ordered by priority:

[options="header",cols="1,2,3"]
|===
|Priority |Quality Goal |Scenario

|1
|Usability
|A new user can register, upload their first image, and apply a filter within 3 minutes without consulting help documentation. (see <<QS_U_1>>)

|2
|Performance Efficiency
|Image upload and thumbnail generation completes within 2 seconds for images up to 10 MB under normal load. (see <<QS_P_1>>)

|3
|Security & Privacy
|All user data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. The system is GDPR-compliant for EU users. (see <<QS_S_1>>)
|===
